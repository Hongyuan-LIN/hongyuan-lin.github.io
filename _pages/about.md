Hello, my name is Hongyuan Lin. I am currently a non-tenured associate professor at the University of Science and Technology of China. My research interests lie in data-driven revenue management and supply chain management, with a particular focus on e-commerce and logistics applications.

Before joining USTC, I received my Ph.D. from the National University of Singapore and my B.S. in Mathematics from the University of Texas at Austin.

Please feel free to contact me at hylin@ustc.edu.cn regarding research discussions, collaborations, or related inquiries.
